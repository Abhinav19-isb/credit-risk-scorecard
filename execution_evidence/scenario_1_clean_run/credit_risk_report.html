6. Business Recommendations

🔴 High-Risk Segment (12.2% — 3,666 customers)

Scores below 580. Reduce credit limits proactively, trigger early collections at 30 DPD, offer structured repayment plans. Highest ROI for collections investment.

🟡 Medium-Risk Segment (Fair — scores 580–670)

Irregular payment behaviour, not yet in default. Send payment reminders 5 days before due date, offer balance transfer at lower rates, monitor monthly for deterioration.

🟢 Low-Risk Segment (66.8% — 20,021 customers)

Scores above 740. Offer proactive credit limit increases, cross-sell premium card products, use as benchmark cohort for new customer acquisition targeting.

📊 Model Monitoring

Retrain quarterly using rolling 12-month window. Monitor PSI monthly — alert if PSI > 0.25. Alert if KS or Gini drops 5+ points from baseline.

7. Assumptions & Limitations

Data Period: Covers April–September 2005 (Taiwan). Recalibrate before deploying in other geographies or time periods.

Class Imbalance: 22.1% default rate handled via class_weight='balanced'. Evaluate SMOTE and threshold optimisation for production deployment.

No Bureau Data: Uses statement and payment data only. Bureau scores and transaction-level features would significantly improve predictive power.

Static Model: Point-in-time scorecard. Requires ongoing monitoring and periodic retraining as customer behaviour evolves.
